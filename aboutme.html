Evin Richard O.

Graduating with a specialization in Mechatronics and Robotics, my fascination with automation and moving parts has been a lifelong passion. As a child, my dream was to create intelligent robots akin to those seen in television shows. Joining ABB as a robotics engineer marked the initial realization of that dream. In my role, I programmed, simulated processes, and performed repairs on robots for two years.

Recognizing the need to infuse intelligence into my creations, I embarked on a journey into Deep Learning for my Ph.D. My focus encompassed image classification, object detection, and computer vision, with an innovative touch of evolutionary processes like neuroevolution. This phase fulfilled the visionary aspect of creating a robot, but the aspiration to enable communication with humans persisted.

The evolution of Natural Language Processing (NLP) in recent years brought me closer to realizing this goal. Post-Ph.D., I directed my focus toward artificial intelligence in NLP, currently applying my expertise in a biotech company. Here, I deploy models on transformers and variational autoencoders to generate sequences.

My journey has been marked by a continuous accumulation of skills and knowledge. Each step has been a building block, laying the foundation for the potential convergence of these varied skills into a unified and impactful project in the future. I am excited about the prospect of weaving together these diverse experiences to contribute meaningfully to the field.
